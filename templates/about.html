About HygionX

HygionX is an AI-assisted health companion that helps you understand your symptoms, assess urgency, and decide when to seek care.

What HygionX does

The system analyses the symptoms you describe in natural language and provides a structured health assessment. It estimates the urgency level, suggests recommended actions, and keeps a timeline of your past assessments for continuity.

  • Understands free‑text symptom descriptions.
  • Highlights potential red‑flag symptoms.
  • Generates follow‑up questions when information is incomplete.
  • Stores sessions so you can review past conversations.

How symptom analysis works

HygionX combines rule‑based medical heuristics with machine‑learning models:

  • Named‑entity recognition to detect medical symptoms in your text.
  • Embedding models to match similar phrasing and recover missed symptoms.
  • Severity scoring based on clinically motivated symptom weights.
  • Red‑flag detection for potentially life‑threatening patterns.

AI triage pipeline overview

  1. You describe how you feel in your own words.
  2. The system extracts and normalises symptoms from your message (and prior messages in the session).
  3. Red‑flag symptoms are checked first; if detected, you receive an emergency guidance message.
  4. If information is incomplete, the LLM generates short follow‑up questions.
  5. When enough information is available, the system scores severity, estimates risk, and recommends next steps.

Important safety notice

HygionX provides guidance only and is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services. Always seek immediate medical attention if you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency.
