How HygionX Works

What Our System Does

  • Analyzes Symptoms: Uses advanced AI to understand your health concerns
  • Provides Insights: Offers potential explanations and health guidance
  • Assesses Urgency: Identifies symptoms that need immediate attention
  • Personalizes Responses: Tailors guidance based on your health history
  • Tracks Progress: Monitors symptoms over time for better insights

Technology Behind It

  • Machine Learning: Trained on vast medical datasets
  • Natural Language Processing: Understands conversational input
  • Medical Knowledge Base: Built on clinical guidelines and research
  • Confidence Scoring: Provides reliability indicators

What HygionX Does NOT Do

Important Medical Disclaimer

HygionX is an AI-powered health assistant designed to provide guidance and information, NOT medical diagnosis or treatment.

System Limitations

  • No Medical Diagnosis: Cannot diagnose diseases or conditions
  • No Prescriptions: Cannot prescribe medications or treatments
  • No Emergency Services: Not for life-threatening emergencies
  • No Lab Tests: Cannot order or interpret medical tests

When to See a Doctor

  • Severe Symptoms: Chest pain, difficulty breathing, severe pain
  • Persistent Issues: Symptoms lasting more than a few days
  • Worsening Condition: Symptoms getting progressively worse
  • Preventive Care: Regular checkups and screenings

Data Privacy & Security

Our Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. Your health information is protected with industry-standard security measures and never shared without your explicit consent.

Data Protection

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Anonymization: Personal data is anonymized for analysis
  • Secure Servers: Data stored on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

Your Rights

  • Transparency: See what data we collect and how it's used
  • Data Export: Download all your personal information anytime
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your data
